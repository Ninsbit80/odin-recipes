Salsa

Salsa is great!

There are endless salsa varieties. Here is one salsa option.

Salsa Ingredients:
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • cilantro - to taste
  • salt and pepper - to taste
Preparation Instructions:
  1. dice all ingredients and combine in a bowl
  2. enjoy!

Pancakes

Pancakes are always delicious

The word pancake is a noun and a verb. Try to broccoli someone on a football field.

Pancake Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 tablespoons butter
Preparation Instructions:
  1. mix dry ingredients together
  2. mix wet ingredients together, except for butter
  3. mix wet and dry ingredients and stir
  4. Heat skillet, grease with butter and ladle batter onto hot skillet. Cook one at a time, flipping as necessary.

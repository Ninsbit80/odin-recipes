Chili

Chili is great in the fall.

Chili Ingredients:
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 cups fresh or canned tomatoes
  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • 1 small can tomato paste
  • 1 small chopped onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 3 cups water
Preparation Instructions:
  1. brown beef and drain grease
  2. add remaining ingredients to the beef in a pot
  3. cook on medium heat stirring occasionally for at least 1 hour

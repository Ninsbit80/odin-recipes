Pizza

Who doesn't like pizza?

Sure, some people have dietary restrictions, but if you can eat it, it's great and versatile!

An Example Pizza Recipe:
  • frozen bread dough
  • fresh tomatoes
  • mozzarella cheese
  • mushrooms
  • basil
  • olives
  • anything else you want
Cooking the pizza!
  1. set the frozen dough to thaw 4-6 hours before dinner time
  2. chop the tomatoes, mushrooms, olives, etc. into bite-size pieces
  3. spread out the dough on a pan
  4. heat oven to 375 degrees
  5. spread tomatoes, cheese and toppings onto pizza
  6. bake until golden brown (time will vary depending on crust's thickness)

Lentil Soup

A healthy and easy meal!

It's always better at a restaurant, but you can actually make it at home!

Lentil Soup Ingredients:
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • small onion
  • 4 cups chicken stock (can substitute vegetable, etc.)
  • 2 cups dry lentils (rinse first)
  • Seasonings (amount to taste):
    • Cumin
    • Black Pepper
    • Turmeric
    • Bay Leaf
Cooking Directions
  1. dice carrots, garlic and onion
  2. saute carrots, garlic and onion briefly
  3. add chicken stock, lentils and Seasonings to the pot
  4. simmer at medium heat for 90 minutes until lentils are soft
  5. remove bay leaf
  6. blend the soup so that it is smooth
  7. enjoy
